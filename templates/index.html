How to get your Deezer ARL Cookie

  1. Open Deezer in your browser (www.deezer.com) and log in to your account.
  2. Open your browser's developer tools. You can usually do this by pressing F12 (or Fn+F12 on some laptops), or by right-clicking on the page and selecting "Inspect" or "Inspect Element".
  3. In the developer tools panel, look for a tab named "Application" (in Chrome, Edge, or Opera) or "Storage" (in Firefox).
  4. In the sidebar of the Application/Storage tab, find the "Cookies" section. Expand it if necessary, and click on the line that shows an address related to Deezer (e.g., https://www.deezer.com).
  5. A table of cookies will appear. Look for a cookie with the name arl in the "Name" column.
  6. Copy the long string of characters from the "Value" column for the arl cookie. This is your ARL cookie value.
